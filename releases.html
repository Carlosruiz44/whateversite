Ruez - Songs for Dreams


A tapestry of textures flowing in and out of lush rhythms and beats that span jazzy type grooves to scattered, industrial like beats. Songs for Dreams is record that will make you wonder as much as it'll make you move.




Boros - Aura


Whatever. continues its meticulously careless releases with a debut EP by Boros. Deep, chunky beats beneath layers of textures, Aura is the type of record for late nights on the dance floor, or at least we think so. Then again, play it whenever you want... Whatever.



Erik Elias & DJ Dandylion - Latinoise EP


Erik Elias & DJ Dandylion's latest offering is nothing short of fun, high octane house sprinkled with latin flavors and dashed with a touch of NYC drama for good measure. Turn it up for this one, the neighbor downstairs will enjoy it.. or not, Whatever.



Mavek - Celestial EP


Jonathan Storm aka Mavek, tailors together spacey yet intimate moods in Celestial EP. A record that suits star gazing as it does cocktails at a candle lit lounge, its sounds will add a little sonic beauty to your special moments.
