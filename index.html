Whatever.


...Whatever.


Whatever is a digital music label based in NYC. From House to Experimental, Techno to Ambient, Deep to Drum & Bass... Whatever. (?/!)

Founded by DJ/Producer Ruez with the intention to release a broad range of sounds that exist in and around (but not limited to) the electronic spectrum. The label was launched with the release of Songs for Dreams, which peaked at #279 on the Juno top 500 downtempo chart.

Whatever has gone on to release 4 EP's in its first year of inception and has a number of new releases set for 2017.
